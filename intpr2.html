Commonly Asked Interview Questions
The following 40 questions, reported by companies that were surveyed by the job portal.com published and copyrighted by the Placement Center.

Interest in Organization


25. What criteria are you using to evaluate the organizations for which you hope to work?


26. What do you know about our organization?


27. Do you have a geographic preference? Why?


28. What do you see as the biggest challenge currently facing organizations such as ours?


29. What are the most important rewards you expect from your career?


30. Why did you decide to seek a position with us?


31. What two or three things are important to you in your job?


32. What other fields/organizations are you interviewing with?


33. Describe the ideal job for you following graduation

 

 

Why Should I Hire You?

34. How have your education and other experiences prepared you for this position?

35. Do you think your grades are a good indication of your academic achievement? Why isn't your GPA higher?

36. Why do you feel we should hire someone with your background?


37. What two accomplishments have given you the most satisfaction?


38. Why should I hire you?


39. What makes you better than the other people I'm seeing today?


40. Describe a contribution you made to the University of Delaware or in your last job.
